About

Hello World!
UBG Online was created as a sucessor to TBG95, another great unblocked games website. Issue is, our school found the site and decided to block most of the games on there. Weirdly enough, they didn't block the actual site, just the games individually. I decided it's probably a good idea to make my own. I had to completely re-learn web development for this, so I hope you like it! If you have any game suggestions, let me know in the contact section! (Or if you know me just tell me irl lol)

Why choose us over TBG95?
We may have less games, but more can come! Our version of Subway Surfers is more updated and a LOT less buggy (won't break when you change skins). We are also leading in customer service. Have a game suggestion? Is something not working? Let us know in the contact section! Unlike TBG95, where you have no way of telling the owner weather or not something is blocked or buggy, we can resolve things for you.
